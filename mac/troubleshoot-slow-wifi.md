# Troubleshoot Slow Wi-Fi on Mac  
*If your Mac is experiencing slow Wi-Fi, follow these steps:*  

1. Restart your router and modem.  
2. Move closer to the Wi-Fi router to reduce interference.  
3. On your Mac, click the Wi-Fi icon and choose "Turn Wi-Fi Off"; wait 10 seconds then turn it back on.  
4. Forget and reconnect to the network: go to System Settings > Network > Wi-Fi > Advanced, select your network, click minus to remove, then reconnect.  
5. Reset NVRAM/PRAM: shut down your Mac, turn it on and immediately press and hold Option+Command+P+R for about 20 seconds.
